History of FC Barcelona

Early Years (1899-1940)

Founded by Joan Gamper, FC Barcelona became a symbol of Catalan identity and sporting excellence.

The Golden Era (1990-2011)

Under the leadership of Johan Cruyff and the brilliance of players like Ronaldinho and Messi, the club enjoyed unprecedented success.

The Modern Era (2012-Present)

With continued excellence and a global fanbase, FC Barcelona remains a dominant force in world football.
